Christina Yoo is a practicing social designer whose passion lies in addressing the needs of those who are marginalized and oppressed in order to work towards more inclusive society.

By collaborating her background in graphic design and social welfare which she studied in her undergrad in South Korea, Christina studied MA in Social Design in Maryland Institute College of Art.

Christina believes in the potential and power of practicing social justice with design as a tool for new and different ways of imagining, doing and making to address injustice and needs in the society.

Sam

She/her, 23, Waitress

Sam was raped by her uncle as a kid and didn't tell anyone until recently.

Sam's best friend is very supportive and wants to be there for her in her recovery.

Sam's family is very close to this uncle and Sam still has to be in the same space with him in family events once a few months but her family doesn't know about this attack.

Sam started going to a support group.

How the experience was + What I would do differently
About the topic of sexual violence
  • Working on the topic around sexual violence as a survivor was personally very meaningful but on one side, retraumatizing. At some point in the process, I completely stopped working on the thesis, because I was too emotionally overwhelmed.
  • This topic is very personal and sensitive. I hope I was mindful and thoughtful enough to work on this topic and in the process did no harm.
  • Like one of my interviewees said, there is no end of the recovery for survivors of sexual violence. What we can do is to learn to cope with what we have been through. It is a slow process, and everybody's pace is different. I somehow feel rushed my recovery to work on this issue.
In the process
  • The level of intervention that I was looking at in the beginning of the project was the macrosystem. I think it was going to be better if I was more mindful about the time frame of this thesis project.
  • When asking questions to the public, questions should be easy to relate and simple. In my brainstorming sessions, I wish I asked questions that are less complicated but closer to individual's emotion and daily life.
  • Connecting with organizations is hard and it takes time. I would try to connect with organizations earlier in the whole process if I do it again.
